Excluded sequences

CoV-GLUE excludes the following GISAID sequences from its analysis:
  • Any sequence of length less than 24000 nucleotides
  • Any sequence with a non-human host, e.g. bat, pangolin
  • Sequences from environmental samples
  • The following sequences marked as having quality issues on GISAID: EPI_ISL_408487, EPI_ISL_408485, EPI_ISL_408483, EPI_ISL_408978, EPI_ISL_412971, EPI_ISL_406799, EPI_ISL_412964, EPI_ISL_412900
  • The following sequences thought to contain sequencing errors: EPI_ISL_406592, EPI_ISL_406595.
