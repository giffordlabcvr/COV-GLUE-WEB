Excluded sequences

CoV-GLUE excludes the following GISAID sequences from its analysis:
  • Any sequence of length less than 29000 nucleotides or greater than 35000
  • Any sequence with a non-human host, e.g. bat, pangolin
  • Sequences from environmental samples
  • Any sequences which cover less than 95% of the coding region
  • Any sequence with more than 10 unique (across the whole dataset) single nucleotide mutations relative to Wuhan-Hu-1.
  • Any sequence which we detect has a frameshifting deletion or insertion relative to Wuhan-Hu-1.
